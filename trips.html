We offer a 5 Nights / 5 day Package for $3,900.00 per person add an extra day of fishing for a total of $4,600.00

Package Includes:

  • Transportation to and from Sitka to Fishermen's Inn
  • 5 days of Lodging and Meals
  • 2 days of Guided Fly Outs
  • 2 days of Guided Saltwater Fishing
  • Fish Processing for up to 100lbs. of fish
  • Fishing Licenses
  • Bed and Sales Taxes
Meals, Lodging and Transportation

All food including a pantry full of snacks is provided. Continental breakfast items such as cereal, fruits and baked goods are there to enjoy. Lunch and a wonderful home cooked dinner, prepared Alaskan family style, are provided for you along with plenty of fresh baked bread and a “bottomless” cookie jar. We also provide daily maid service, a private hot tub and air transportation between Sitka and Fishermen’s Inn.

Guided Freshwater Fly-in Fishing

Delight in a day of guided fly in fishing with Kevin, a licensed guide with the forest service, and a licensed sport-fishing guide with the State of Alaska. Be flown into mountain lakes and streams for rainbow and cutthroat trout or bays to fish for all 5 species of salmon.

Guided Saltwater Fishing

Enjoy a full day of guided saltwater fishing with Glen of Chatham Strait Charters for Halibut, Salmon, Lingcod and a large variety of Rockfish. Fish processing includes, filleted, vacuum packaged, blast frozen and boxed fish.

Skiff with Outboard Motor

We provide a 17 ft. aluminum skiff with a 40hp outboard. All fishing poles and gear are provided as well as safety equipment (radio, flares, life jackets, etc...)

We can also book a custom package around our guests particular interests.

All rates are based on a 2 person minimum.

We have 4 single and 2 double ocean kayaks available for all of our guests to enjoy.
