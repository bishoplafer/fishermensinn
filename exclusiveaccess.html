Fishermen’s Inn offers access to world class fishing destinations! Here you can hike in the alpine and on beaches in the same day! With Fishermen’s Inn in Port Alexander Alaska you have the opportunity to fish unbelievable locations. Your guide and pilot Kevin Mulligan has decades (35 years!) of sport and commercial fishing experience.

Not many people have the opportunity to experience fishing in pristine rivers and in such a productive ocean environment. Fishermen’s Inn provides the warm welcome so often missing in wilderness fishing lodges. Our guests come back over and over for more great fishing and Alaskan adventure! Imagine savoring your fresh fish back home thanks to our commercial-grade blast freezer and vacuum packer. Halibut, king, coho, and so much more await you in the bountiful Southeast Alaskan Sea!
