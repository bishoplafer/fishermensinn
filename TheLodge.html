The Lodge

  1. Home
  2. The Lodge

Fisherman or Fisherwoman, we take them all! In Alaska, good food makes good times. Karen serves it up hot! Don’t plan on losing weight on this vacation!

Fishermen’s Inn lodge is off the grid, but still comfy and cozy. We have Select Comfort beds and nice covered decks to relax on. There is also a hot tub and of course we provide those nice white, thick, robes. A washer and dryer are also accessible so you can pack light and take more fish home as luggage! Digital satellite television and satellite telephone are at the ready if you want them. There is an iMac for checking your e-mail.

Fishermen’s Inn sports an amazing separate fish house to vacuum-pack and freeze your prized catch with a professional blast freezer. A smoker is available. Kayaks are provided for all guests. There are fantastic places to paddle to see wildlife and ship wrecks.

Fishermen’s Inn is near the beach yet tucked away and secluded! A perfect place to get away in comfort and feel the raw frontier at the same time! Our covered deck is perfect for outdoor entertaining or just relaxing on the edge of true wilderness.

The adjacent jacuzzi hot tub is a wonderful end of day treat for the mind and body. Enjoy the privacy of this serene little fishing community! Fishermen's Inn has an entertainment system with DSS satellite television and music that is provided for your enjoyment. Upstairs you will find a master suite with a private bath, Select Comfort queen size bed, a loft with twin sized mattress and an office area. A smaller room is also available with a semi-private bath, twin Select Comfort beds, and a loft with another twin mattress.

There is also a stand alone cabin! It is very private and quiet. Clean, cozy, and well equipped. Accommodations like this are very difficult to construct in such a remote setting. That also explains why Fishermen’s Inn is such a rare find! All the hard work has been done by The Mulligan’s show through in every detail.

All that is left to do is relax and enjoy your vacation!
